About Me - Kelina Cowell

I am an aspiring Game QA Tester in the UK, building experience through self directed portfolio projects on shipped games for PC Game Pass and mobile. I enjoy the practical side of QA: planning sessions, running focused tests, and writing bug reports that are clear enough for a developer to pick up and act on.

I have not worked in a studio yet. Most of my hands on experience comes from my Game Academy Bootcamp projects and my own portfolio work. Before that I worked in emergency control, logistics and contact centres. Those roles trained me to stay calm when things break, follow procedures accurately, and keep clean written records. All of that transfers nicely into QA.

In my projects I define scope and environment, design simple test plans or exploratory charters, capture video or screenshots for every significant run, and log issues in Jira style boards. My bug reports always include a short summary, exact repro steps, expected vs actual result, environment, repro rate, and why the issue matters to the player.

I am currently building a portfolio that covers different testing focuses such as functional checks, light regression on live builds, exploratory and edge case testing, basic accessibility and comfort checks, and cross platform work that includes VR. Each case study is small on purpose so that a hiring manager can see my thought process without needing an hour to read it.
