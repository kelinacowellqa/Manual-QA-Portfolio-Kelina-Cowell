Exploratory QA & Live Testing

This section focuses on exploratory QA, destructive testing, and real-world testing scenarios across game jams, demos, alpha, and beta builds.

Unlike structured case studies, this work reflects shorter, fast-paced testing sessions where the goal is to quickly identify impactful issues, validate player experience, and adapt testing approaches in unstable or evolving builds.

The emphasis here is on practical bug hunting, adaptability, and applying QA thinking in less controlled environments.
