Exploratory QA & Live Testing

This section collects exploratory QA practice and live testing work across smaller, faster-moving, and less controlled environments than my main case studies.

It includes destructive testing, game jam QA, demos, alpha and beta builds, and other short-form projects where the focus is on quick bug discovery, practical judgement, and adapting to unstable or evolving builds.

The goal here is not depth for its own sake, but evidence of consistency: regular hands-on testing, clear reporting, and QA thinking applied outside longer structured project work.
